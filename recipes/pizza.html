Pizza

This is a Pizza!

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Pizza Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Chicken
  • Cilantro
  • Diced Tomatoes

Steps

  1. Spread the Dough and spread the Pizza Sauce leaving just the edges.
  2. Sprinkle the Shredded Cheese, Chopped Chicken, Cilantro, and the Diced Tomatoes around evenly.
  3. Cook in the oven at 350°F for 15 minutes.
  4. Slice and enjoy!
