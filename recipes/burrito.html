Burrito

These are burritos!

Ingredients

  • Tortilla
  • Beans
  • Sour Cream
  • Shredded Cheese
  • Chopped Lettuce
  • Chopped Tomato
  • Avacado
  • Sauces

Steps

  1. Lay the Tortilla on the plate.
  2. Pour the Beans on the Tortilla.
  3. Spread the Sour Cream and Avacado on top.
  4. Sprinkle some Shredded Cheese.
  5. Add the chopped Lettuce and Tomatoes.
  6. Spread your favorite Sauces.
